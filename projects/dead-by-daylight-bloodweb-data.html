Here, "item" refers to any unlockable, as opposed to the items that are exclusive to Survivors.

Inner ring nodes are those connected directly to the central node. Middle ring nodes are connected to the inner ring nodes but are not the central node. Outer ring nodes are connected to the middle ring nodes but are not part of the inner ring; they are located at the ends of each bloodweb.

Perks override the maximum rarity limit they are associated with. For example, it is possible for one rare item and two rare perks to coexist, even if the maximum number of rare items allowed in that specific bloodweb is set to "1".

The Entity starts consuming nodes after you purchase six nodes from bloodweb levels 10 to 15 (except at bloodweb level 11, where it is seven nodes), and after you purchase five nodes for all subsequent bloodweb levels.

The item probability tables assume that no prestige modifiers have been applied.
