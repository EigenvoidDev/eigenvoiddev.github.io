I'm a data miner, programmer, and reverse engineer with a passion for understanding how video games function behind the scenes. Over the past decade, I've explored a wide range of titles, including Brawlhalla, the Call of Duty series, Castle Crashers, Dead by Daylight, New World, Star Wars Battlefront, and Warframe.

My work involves data extraction, reverse engineering, custom scripting, and building tools that reveal the hidden systems behind games. I aim to promote transparency in game design and help players better understand the mechanics driving their favorite titles.
