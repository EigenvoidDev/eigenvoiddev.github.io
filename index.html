About Me

I'm Eigenvoid, a data miner, programmer, and reverse engineer with a passion for exploring the inner workings of video games. Over the past decade, I've explored a wide range of titles, including Brawlhalla, the Call of Duty series, Castle Crashers, Dead by Daylight, New World, Star Wars Battlefront, and Warframe. My work has involved extracting and analyzing game data, developing software applications, writing custom scripts tailored to specific game functions, and reverse engineering systems to uncover their underlying mechanics. My goal has always been to inform the public about how video games work on a more technical level, promoting transparency and helping players better understand the systems that drive their favorite games.

In addition to this, I have years of experience competing in platform fighting game tournaments. I achieved notable success in Brawlhalla, including becoming a BCX Finalist and securing four top 32 finishes in official esports tournaments. Additionally, I was invited to compete in VGBootCamp's Justice League: Road To EVO Invitational due to my status as a top MultiVersus player during its open beta. My experiences have not only sharpened my competitive skills but also deepened my understanding of what it takes to excel at the highest levels of esports.
